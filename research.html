Deep Learning for Wound Tissue Segmentation: A Comprehensive Evaluation Using a Novel Dataset

2nd of 5 authors — Kabir, M.A., Roy, N., Hossain, M.E., Featherston, J., Ahmed, S.
Research question
How do patch-based, super-pixel-based, and full segmentation models compare for wound-tissue segmentation?
Dataset
147 wound images across six tissue types: slough, granulation, maceration, necrosis, bone, and tendon.
Approach
Comparative evaluation including UNet, cGAN-based methods, and FPN+VGG16 across three labelling formats.
My contribution
Literature review and coding implementation.

Enhancing Offensive Bengali Social Media Meme Detection: A Weighted Ensemble Architecture

Dataset creation & modelling

Earlier work in multimodal fusion — combining text and image models — that informs my current approach to combining imaging and clinical modalities. Not part of my medical-imaging research line, included here as methodological background.

Approach
Weighted ensemble of multimodal (BERT + image classifier) models across a three-level hierarchical classification.
Dataset
Multi-modal Offensive Memes Bengali Dataset (MoMBD), 3,662 memes, created for this work.
Outcome
Weighted F1-score of 71.84% on fine-grained classification.

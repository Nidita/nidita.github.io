Diagnostic study
Manuscript in preparation · targeting MICAD 2026

What Limits Continual Learning for Abdominal-Trauma CT Detection?

This study benchmarks fine-tuning, EWC, Replay, and EWC+Replay, then asks whether weak performance is really caused by forgetting. The analysis traces near-chance results to representation quality and coarse patient-level labeling, motivating a multiple-instance learning reformulation.
